How Assessments Work

Module Quiz
  • Attached to a specific module
  • Fires automatically after the learner marks that module complete
  • Pass or fail — learner continues to the next module either way
  • Result shown for self-check; no certificate issued
  • One quiz per module maximum
Final Exam
  • Fires after all modules are completed
  • Passing the exam marks the training as completed
  • Certificate is automatically issued on pass
  • Failing allows retry up to max attempts
  • One active Final Exam per training is recommended
