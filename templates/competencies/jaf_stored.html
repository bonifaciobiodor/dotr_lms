Introduction

This form is intended to gather comprehensive information on the job roles, responsibilities, and required competencies of employees within a specific office, service, or division. The data collected will primarily support competency framework validation and also serve as a reference for other human resource functions such as workforce planning, training needs analysis, and performance management.

Objectives

  • Document the main and secondary responsibilities of each position.
  • Identify skills and proficiency levels required for effective performance.
  • Provide a basis for competency gap analysis and targeted development programs.
  • Ensure alignment between job roles and organizational objectives.
