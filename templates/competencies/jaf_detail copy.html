Introduction

This form is intended to gather comprehensive information on the job roles, responsibilities, and required competencies of employees within a specific office, service, or division. Objectives

  • Document the main and secondary responsibilities of each position.
  • Identify skills and proficiency levels required for effective performance.
  • Provide a basis for competency gap analysis and targeted development programs.
  • Ensure alignment between job roles and organizational objectives.
